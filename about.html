Our Story

Made with love, crafted with care

A mother and daughter passion project, bringing bespoke wedding touches to life from our studio in beautiful Mallorca.

Who We Are

Nicky & Alyssa — the team behind the magic

At LingaLonga Art, we believe that your wedding should be as unique and memorable as your love story. As a mother and daughter team, we pour creativity, care, and a touch of magic into every piece of bespoke wedding decor and personalised signage we craft.

What began as a shared passion for capturing special moments has grown into a dedication to helping couples create celebrations infused with personality and beauty. Whether it's an elegant welcome sign, charming table numbers, or a keepsake you'll treasure for years, we work closely with each couple, bringing their dream details to life.

From modern elegance to rustic romance, every handmade piece is designed to reflect your style and celebrate your journey together. Based in the heart of Mallorca, we are lucky to be surrounded by some of the most beautiful wedding venues in the world — and that beauty inspires everything we create.

"Our promise: heartfelt artistry, made with love, that adds joy and meaning to your big day."
