FAQs

Questions we get asked a lot

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend getting in touch at least 4 months before your wedding date. For large orders or peak season (May–October in Mallorca) we'd suggest 6-8 months to be safe.

Do you ship outside of Mallorca?

Absolutely! We ship across Spain and Europe regularly. International shipping is available too — just let us know your location and we'll confirm costs and timelines.

Can I see a proof before you make it?

Yes, always! We send a digital proof for your approval before we begin making your piece. We want you to be completely happy with the design before we start.

What if I want something not listed in the shop?

Everything we do is bespoke, so if you can dream it, we'll do our best to make it! Just get in touch and describe what you're looking for.

How do I pay?

We accept payment via PayPal. A 50% deposit is required to begin your order, with the balance due before dispatch.

What's your cancellation policy?

As all items are bespoke and made to order, deposits are non-refundable once production has begun. Please contact us as soon as possible if your circumstances change.
