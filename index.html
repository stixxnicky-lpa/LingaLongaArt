Our Story

A mother & daughter passion project

At LingaLonga Art, we believe that your wedding should be as unique and memorable as your love story. As a mother and daughter team, Nicky and Alyssa, we pour creativity, care, and a touch of magic into every piece of bespoke wedding decor and personalised signage we craft.

What began as a shared passion for capturing special moments has grown into a dedication to helping couples create celebrations infused with personality and beauty.
